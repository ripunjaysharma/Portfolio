Video Editing Portfolio: Celebrating Independence Day for Rising India

During my tenure as a Video Editor at Rising India from July to August 2022, I had the privilege of independently composing a captivating video to commemorate Independence Day. Leveraging my expertise in DaVinci Resolve 18, I seamlessly blended over 100 clips to craft a visually engaging narrative that resonated with the essence of the occasion.

Some key takeaways are :
Independence Day Video Composition: I conceptualized and executed the entire video project from start to finish. This included selecting appropriate footage, editing transitions, adding effects, and fine-tuning the overall presentation to ensure a polished and professional output.
Platform Outreach: The final video was strategically posted on multiple platforms to maximize its reach and impact. It was featured on Rising India's LinkedIn Page, boasting an audience of over 44,000 followers. Additionally, it gained visibility on the Treeandhumanknot LinkedIn Page with 9,000+ followers and was also uploaded on YouTube for broader accessibility.
Collaboration and Coordination: Effective communication and collaboration were pivotal in this project's success. I liaised with multiple leaders and stakeholders to grasp their vision and requirements for the video. Despite a tight deadline of 20 days, I efficiently managed the workflow, ensuring timely delivery without compromising on quality.

Our project, which achieved the impressive 4th Rank out of over 300 participants, is a cutting-edge career prediction system. The core of this system lies in its sophisticated use of machine learning models such as Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, and XGBoost. These models were meticulously implemented to accurately forecast future career options and pathways for individuals who are navigating their professional journeys. One of the standout features of our project is the development of a user-friendly interface using MongoDB and ReactJS. This interface seamlessly integrates with our machine learning models, allowing users to input their data and receive detailed predictions and recommendations. Among these models, the Decision Tree model played a significant role as a challenger, enriching the predictions with comprehensive explanations and insights. Overall, our project not only demonstrates technical prowess in machine learning and web development but also showcases a practical application of these technologies in providing valuable guidance and clarity for individuals exploring their career paths.
